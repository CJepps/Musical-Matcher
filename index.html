Welcome to Music Matcher!

Welcome to music Matcher! This game of skill is aimed at improving your memory and even helping with your musical sight reading! Simply match identical cards until there are none left to match. Higher scores are reached by making fewer moves but make sure you finish matching cards before the time limit runs out or you will lose! Good luck :)
